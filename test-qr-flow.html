Testing QR Code Flow

Open the TruCycle app and follow these steps:

  1. Sign up/Sign in with any user credentials
  2. Complete the onboarding process
  3. Click on the Messages button in the header
  4. Select one of the sample conversations
  5. Click the "Generate QR Code" button in the quick actions
  6. Select your role (donor or collector)
  7. Generate the QR code
  8. Test the download and share functionality
